Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense data you choose to enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and personalise the Spendly service for you. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling you to filter and manage your transactions. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party services. We use Google Fonts to load typefaces; this request is made from your browser directly to Google's servers and is subject to Google's own privacy policy. No expense data or account information is shared in this process.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided in the app. We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
